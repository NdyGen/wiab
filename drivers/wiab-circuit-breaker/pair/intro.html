Circuit Breaker

Circuit Breakers provide hierarchical flow control for your Homey automations. Like electrical circuit breakers, they can be turned ON (allowing flows) or OFF (blocking flows).

Key Feature: Circuit breakers can be organized in a parent-child hierarchy. When a parent breaker is turned OFF, all of its child breakers automatically turn OFF as well.
  • Hierarchical Control: Organize breakers in a tree structure (e.g., "House" → "Floor 1" → "Living Room")
  • Cascade Behavior: Turning OFF a parent automatically turns OFF all descendants
  • Independent Children: Children can still be controlled independently regardless of parent state
  • No Cycles: The system prevents circular dependencies automatically
  • Root Breakers: Breakers without a parent act as top-level controls
