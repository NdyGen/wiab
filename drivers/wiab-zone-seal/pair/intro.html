Zone Seal

Zone Seal monitors door and window contact sensors in a zone to determine if the zone is properly sealed (all doors and windows closed) or leaky (at least one opening detected).

Key Feature: Aggregates multiple contact sensors to provide a single sealed/leaky status, perfect for security checks, climate control, or automation triggers.
  • Sealed: All monitored doors and windows are closed
  • Leaky: At least one door or window is open
  • Stale Detection: Identifies sensors that haven't reported in a while, indicating potential battery or connectivity issues
  • Zone-Based: Automatically discovers all contact sensors in the selected Homey zone
  • Flow Triggers: Trigger flows when zone becomes sealed, leaky, or when stale sensors are detected

In the next step, you'll select a Homey zone to monitor. All contact sensors in that zone will be automatically included.
